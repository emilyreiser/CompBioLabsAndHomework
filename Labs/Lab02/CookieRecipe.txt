Best Chocolate Chip Cookies

1.75 cups unbleached all-purpose flour, (8 3/4 ounces)
0.5 teaspoon baking soda
1.75 sticks butter
0.5 cup sugar
0.75 cups packed dark brown sugar
1 teaspoon salt
2 teaspoons vanilla 
1 egg
1 egg yolk
1.25 cups chocolate chips

Steps: 
1. Mix dry ingredients
2. Mix wet ingredients
3. Mix all ingredients
4. Roll into small balls and put on non-stick pan
5. Bake at 425 degreed F for 12 minutes
